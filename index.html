Allah Help Those Who Help Themselves

Our Vision

Our Vision is to inspire an appreciation of Islamic values ​​by educating the community about the religion and its principles and demonstrating their effectiveness in today's world.

Our Mission

The Mission of Jama Masjid Magurjan (1) To provide a safe and comfortable facility. (2) Namaz and interaction with the Muslim community in accordance with the teachings of the Holy Quran and Sunnah.
